## Reporting a Vulnerability

Security is a high priority for PSA Business Suite. If you discover a vulnerability, please do NOT report it publicly or open an issue on GitHub. 

Instead, please send an email to **dev.psajewelry@gmail.com**.

Please include:
*   Detailed steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
*   A description of the impact.
*   Any potential mitigations you suggest.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide a timeline for the resolution.
